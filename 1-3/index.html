Welcome to Brewed Awakening!

At Brewed Awakening, we're passionate about coffee. We believe that a great cup of coffee can make all the difference in your day, and we're committed to bringing you the best possible coffee experience.

Whether you're looking for a classic cup of joe, a latte, or something a little more adventurous, we've got you covered. Our baristas are trained to craft the perfect cup of coffee, every time.

But we're more than just a coffee shop. We're a community. We believe in building connections and fostering relationships, and we're committed to being a welcoming space for everyone who walks through our doors.
